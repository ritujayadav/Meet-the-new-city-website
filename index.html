VIRAR

Virar is a popular city in Maharashtra, India, part of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region.Despite being in close proximity to the ever busy lifestyle of Mumbai, Virar manages to be a well known tourist place for many reason,Come explore this unique city. With exciting activities and events taking place all year round there is truly something for everyone, no matter your age or interest. We look forward to welcoming you!

TAKMAK FORT

The trek is a peek into the remnants of a history, and stands on a very steep hill at about 2000 feet. which is fully surrounded by a dense forest.

BASSEIN FORT

The Portuguese established Bassein fort.The fort is a monument of national importance and is protected by the Archaeological Survey of India.

TUNGARESHWAR NATIONAL PARK

It is a widlife santuary which holds rare flora and fauna species which is 665 meters above sea level, making it a very popular destination for trekkers.

JIVDANI TEMPLE

It is the most significant landmark in Virar located on the mountain. It is famous throughout the country for its temple of Goddess Jivdani and located 1375 steps above ground.
